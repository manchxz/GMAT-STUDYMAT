The Flashcards

Two decks: formulas and traps. Click the card to flip. Use the keyboard — Space flips, Arrow keys navigate. Spaced-recall friendly: cycle until everything feels boring.

Formulas 20 cards Traps 12 cards Recommended use 5 minutes / day

Definition
click to flip back

A card is "learned" when you can recall the back without flipping. Cycle through, marking nothing. When the deck feels boring, you have learned it.
